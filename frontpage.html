The Club Sandwich

Ingredients

Ingredients Portion Size
Bread 3 Slices
Mayonnaise 1 tbsp
Eggs 1
Tomatoes 1
Chicken Slices Or Turkey Breasts 2 or 3
Salad Leaves 6 leaves

Recipe steps

  • 1: Heat grill to High and cook the bacon, turning halfway through, until crisp. Toast the bread and spread one slice with the mayo.
  • 2: Layer on the egg, tomato and bacon, then top with another slice of bread. Add the chicken or turkey, bacon, then lettuce. Finish with the final slice of bread.
